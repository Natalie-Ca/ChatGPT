About the Author

Argent Morrin

Argent Morrin is a medical expert working in the drug development industry, with a background spanning cardiovascular surgery, academia, and applied behavioral science. Their experience includes leadership roles in medical education and organ transplantation, as well as ongoing work with language models and artificial intelligence.

Morrin’s work explores the boundaries between natural and artificial cognition—less out of fascination, more out of a persistent need to understand systems from within. Their projects, including non fiction narrative writing and ongoing research project, examine how human reasoning interacts with machine-generated language and where that interaction starts to blur.

Based in Ireland, Morrin continues to work at the intersection of clinical research and automation, focusing on areas where technology influences decision-making, identity, and communication.
